Lovina

Written by detapranata on 15 May 2024

Lovina or often called Lovina Beach is one of the marine tourist areas in the northern part of the island of Bali. This beach, which is located in Lovina in the North Bali area, is indeed interesting because it is still relatively natural. One of the unique things about this beach is that you can watch the action of wild dolphins in the sea. Lovina is located in North Bali on the north coast of Bali Island, approximately 10 km west of Singaraja. Lovina Beach is in Kalibukbuk Village, Buleleng District, Buleleng Regency, Bali. Because of this, sometimes people call it the Kalibukbuk tourist area. Lovina Beach, which has black sand, is still natural, making it interesting to visit. What's interesting about Lovina Beach is the trip to the middle of the sea in Lovina waters. You can find dolphins in Lovina waters which are located about 1 kilometer from the beach. The Bali Sea in Lovina waters is relatively calm so you can tour the sea using a fishing boat.
