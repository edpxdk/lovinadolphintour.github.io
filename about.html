Nyoman Rena Yasa is the owner of Renayasa Dolphin. He has been running this business since 2007. Nyoman Rena Yasa lives in Celukbuluh Village, Banjar Dinas Kalibukbuk, Buleleng, Bali. As an experienced tour guide, especially in dolphin watching tours, he is known for his expertise in exploring the waters around Lovina and finding places where dolphins often congregate. Nyoman Rena Yasa has become the main choice for tourists who want to get this unique experience. Nyoman Rena Yasa is known not only as an informative tour guide, but also as someone who cares about the welfare of dolphins. He ensures that interactions with dolphins are carried out safely and responsibly, prioritizing nature conservation and the continuity of the dolphin's habitat. In addition, his expertise in providing explanations about dolphin behavior and habitat adds value to the tourism experience he offers. Friendly and enthusiastic, Rena Yasa not only guides tourists to see dolphins, but also to understand how important environmental preservation is for the existence of these creatures. As a dolphin tour guide in Lovina, Nyoman Rena Yasa has created an unforgettable experience for visitors who come to enjoy the natural beauty and unique marine life in Bali.
